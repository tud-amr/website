We’re thrilled to share that our paper, “Agile and Cooperative Aerial Manipulation of a Cable-Suspended Load,” has been published in [Science Robotics](https://www.science.org/doi/abs/10.1126/scirobotics.adu8015). This work was led by Dr. Sihao Sun as part of his [Veni project](https://autonomousrobots.nl/projects/veni-sihao).
